Privacy Policy

Introduction
CalSyncMaster respects the privacy of its users and is committed to protecting their personal data. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose information obtained from users through our extension.
Collection and Use of Information
The CalSyncMaster extension uses the following Google Calendar API permissions:
https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events.readonly https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar
We access your Google Calendar to analyze your schedule and suggest optimal planning. We do not modify your calendar data without your permission.
Data Storage
We do not store personal data on our servers. All data processed by the extension is used only during its active usage and is not retained after the session ends.
Disclosure of Information
We do not disclose personal data to third parties, except as required by law.
Security
We take all necessary measures to protect the collected information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
Using Google User Data
The use and transfer of information received from Google APIs by CalSyncMaster adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We commit to not transferring information received from Google to other apps or entities, except as part of providing the functionalities of CalSyncMaster and as outlined in this privacy policy.
Changes to the Privacy Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on the extension page.
